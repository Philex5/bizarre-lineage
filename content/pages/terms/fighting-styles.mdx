# Fighting Styles in Bizarre Lineage

Fighting styles are combat layers that work alongside your stand and affect how your build plays in real fights.

## Why fighting styles matter

The right style can improve pressure, mobility, coverage, or consistency. That means a build should be judged by the stand and style together, not by the stand alone.

## When players usually optimize styles

Styles matter early, but most players should first secure a workable stand and stop obvious stat mistakes before over-optimizing style choices.

## Common fighting style mistake

The usual mistake is copying a style from a top-tier build without checking whether the rest of the setup supports it.
