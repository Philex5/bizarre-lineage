# Awakening in Bizarre Lineage

Awakening is a later-stage progression term used for stronger upgrade paths and more advanced build planning.

## Where awakening fits

Awakening is not usually the first system a new player should worry about. It sits later in the route, after you already understand your stand, stats, and progression basics.

## Why awakening matters

Players care about awakening because it can raise a build's ceiling, but it only pays off when the rest of the route is already in good shape.

## Common awakening mistake

The usual mistake is reading awakening guides too early instead of fixing more immediate problems like bad stat spending or a weak first stand.
