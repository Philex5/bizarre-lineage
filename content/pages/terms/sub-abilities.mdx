# Sub-Abilities in Bizarre Lineage

Sub-abilities are extra layers added to a build to improve coverage, utility, or specific matchups.

## Why sub-abilities matter

A stand is not the whole build. Sub-abilities can patch weaknesses, sharpen a role, or make a setup feel much more complete.

## When to care about sub-abilities

Most players should understand their main stand and stat logic first. Sub-abilities become more important once the basic route is already stable.

## Common sub-ability mistake

The usual mistake is copying a flashy setup without understanding whether it actually fits the stand and route being played.
