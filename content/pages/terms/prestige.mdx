# Prestige in Bizarre Lineage

Prestige is a reset-style progression system. It matters because it can open stronger long-term progress, but only if you use it at the right time.

## What prestige means

Prestige is not just a button you press the moment it appears. It is a route decision. Good prestige timing depends on what you gain next and what you still need from your current run.

## When prestige is worth it

Prestige usually makes sense when:

- your current route is slowing down
- key value is already secured
- you understand what the reset helps you unlock next

## Common prestige mistake

The most common mistake is prestiging just because it is available, then realizing your next route is weaker or less prepared than expected.
