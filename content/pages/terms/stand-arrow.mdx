# Stand Arrow in Bizarre Lineage

The Stand Arrow is one of the core items used to obtain many stands in Bizarre Lineage.

## Why the Stand Arrow matters

For many players, the Stand Arrow is where the first stand route really begins. It shapes early decisions about whether to keep a usable stand, reroll, or start planning around a better target.

## How players should think about it

Treat the Stand Arrow as part of a route, not just a random gamble. A usable result can be worth more than endlessly chasing a premium stand too early.

## Common Stand Arrow mistake

The common mistake is burning too many resources on rerolls before the account has a stable build or progression plan.
