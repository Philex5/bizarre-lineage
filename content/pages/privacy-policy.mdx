## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Bizarre Lineage Wiki collects, uses, and protects information when you browse the site. The site is an independent, content-first game guide website focused on English-language pages for Bizarre Lineage. It is not affiliated with Roblox or the game developers.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect limited information to operate and improve the site:

1. **Usage Data**
   - **What We Collect**: Basic analytics data such as page views, referral sources, device type, browser type, and general interaction patterns.
   - **Purpose**: To understand which guides are useful, improve page structure, and monitor site performance.

2. **Technical Data**
   - **What We Collect**: Standard server and request information such as IP address, approximate location derived from network data, response logs, and error events.
   - **Purpose**: To secure the site, diagnose issues, and maintain reliability.

3. **Cookies and Similar Storage**
   - **What We Collect**: Small files or browser storage used for analytics, theme preference, and basic site functionality.
   - **Purpose**: To improve usability and understand aggregate behavior.

4. **Direct Contact Information**
   - **What We Collect**: If you contact the site owner directly, we may receive your email address and the contents of your message.
   - **Purpose**: To respond to questions, corrections, or legal requests.

## Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we process. No Internet transmission or storage system is guaranteed to be completely secure, so absolute security cannot be promised.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only in limited situations such as:

- With hosting, analytics, and infrastructure providers that help operate the site.
- To comply with legal obligations or lawful requests.
- To protect the rights, property, and safety of the site owner, users, or the public.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the effective date at the top of this page will be updated.
