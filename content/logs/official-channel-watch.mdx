- The official Roblox experience is live at [Bizarre Lineage on Roblox](https://www.roblox.com/games/14890802310/Bizarre-Lineage).

- Public reporting on a Trello board is mixed, so we are treating Discord and the Roblox game page as the safer baseline until the board status is fully consistent.

For this site, that means each card on the Updates page will summarize what those official-facing channels are signaling instead of guessing at unverified patch notes.
