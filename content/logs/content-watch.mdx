Beyond balance changes, the next most important update pattern is new content.

- Pro Game Guides highlights patch notes, new stand releases, and upcoming content as the kinds of posts players should expect from the official channels.
- That gives this site a clear editorial job: each future update card can explain what was added, who the content is for, and whether it changes the farming or progression priority list.
- Raid adjustments and limited event rewards are especially worth tracking because they can change what materials, bosses, or group routes are worth running.

Once official notes are clearer, we can turn these cards into sharper summaries for specific stands, raids, and event windows.
