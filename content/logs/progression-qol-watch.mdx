Not every meaningful update is a new stand or a dramatic nerf.

- Smaller changes to drop flow, boss access, inventory friction, or leveling pace can have a big effect on beginner routing.
- Public writeups keep emphasizing that players use official update channels to stay current, which usually means there is regular patch movement even when the changes are less flashy.
- For a guide-first site like this one, the useful part is not just listing the tweak. It is translating the tweak into practical advice: what to farm first, what to skip, and whether prestige timing changes.

That is why the Updates page can work well here. It gives us a place to convert scattered official notes into quick, readable action items.
