The clearest pattern across public coverage is that balance patches matter more than almost anything else in Bizarre Lineage.

- Destructoid specifically calls out the need to keep up with buffs, nerfs, and stand or spec changes because the game is progression-heavy and PvP-sensitive.
- That lines up with how players usually experience new patches: a stand can jump in value quickly, while an old farming route becomes less efficient after a tuning pass.
- When we add future update cards, expect balance summaries to focus on tier movement, stat-routing changes, and whether a patch helps beginners, grinders, or duel-focused players the most.

This page is a good fit for those summaries because a short timeline card is faster to scan than rechecking multiple channels every session.
